## About Me

I am an undergraduate student at Hunan University, majoring in Computer Science and Technology.

I am interested in Computer Graphics. Specifically, I have participated in research projects related to parametric curves, differentiable vector graphics rendering, and Gaussian splatting. I am also looking forward to exploring more topics in CG, such as illumination, rendering, geometry processing, etc.

I also enjoy music and video games in my free time. I served as president of Hunan University Student Chorus during 2024–2025.
